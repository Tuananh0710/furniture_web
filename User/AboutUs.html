At LOOM, we believe that furniture is more than objects—it's the fabric of life, woven together with craftsmanship, sustainability, and timeless design.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, LOOM began with a simple vision: to create furniture that brings warmth, elegance, and meaning to every home. Our name reflects our philosophy—each piece is carefully woven together, combining artisan craftsmanship with sustainable materials.

From our workshop in Portland, Oregon, we've grown into a trusted name in contemporary furniture design. Every piece tells a story of dedication, from the sustainably-sourced wood to the hands that shape it.

Today, LOOM continues to honor traditional woodworking techniques while embracing modern design sensibilities, creating furniture that stands the test of time both in durability and style.

What We Stand For
