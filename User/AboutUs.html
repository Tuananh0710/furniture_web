Introduce about Loom

We were founded with a desire to enhance the quality of living spaces for Vietnamese families and to elevate awareness of Vietnamese-made products with export-level quality. VFurniture is passionate and dedicated to bringing the finest indoor and outdoor furniture products that are safe, non-toxic, and of exceptional quality to every Vietnamese home.

Who We Are

VFurniture specializes in exporting high-end furniture lines to markets in Europe and the United States. As the exclusive representative of over 10 premium furniture brands, we aim to satisfy those who appreciate elegant and distinctive lifestyles.

Visiting our showroom, you can explore and experience luxurious European-style interior design spaces with premium Indoor and Outdoor products from renowned brands such as MODLOFT, ARTICLE, BOMBAY, and SUOFEIYA.

VFurniture guarantees that all our products meet Nontoxic standards (lead-free paints) — safe for both the environment and human health — and are certified by the FSC – Forest Stewardship Council.

Moreover, VFurniture is also a trusted provider of interior design and construction services in Nha Be. With years of experience in the industry, we bring customers modern, convenient, and aesthetic living and working spaces that reflect their unique style.
